Love, Your Trans Big Sister

Advice on the Transfeminine Journey

Love, Your Trans Big Sister is a heartfelt, practical handbook for transfeminine or questioning individuals (and those who love them). Blending personal stories with actionable advice and journaling prompts, the book offers support, clarity, and encouragement for anyone navigating identity and learning to trust themselves.
