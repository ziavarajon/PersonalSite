Biography

Zia Varajon is a Chicago-based author and creative whose work centers on self-trust, personal growth, and navigating identity with confidence. Through her writing, Zia explores themes of boundaries, intuition, and emotional resilience, offering guidance that is both deeply personal and widely relatable.

Her debut book, Love, Your Trans Big Sister: Advice on the Transfeminine Journey, is a heartfelt, practical guide to trusting your gut and embracing your truest self. Blending humor, honesty, and lived experience, Zia creates a voice that feels like a trusted “big sister” who has been there, done that. Outside of her writing, Zia is passionate about creative expression, spoiling her cats (Halloumi & Mothman), and building community through shared experiences.
